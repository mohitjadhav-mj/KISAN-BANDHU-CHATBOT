Welcome!

This is the home page of a chatbot. Here's a quick guide on how to use our helpful chatbot to get information about crops and farming practices:

Steps of Using chatbot:

  1. The Chatbot is on the bottom Right Corner of the web page.
  2. Start a Conversation: Simply say "Hi" or "Hello" to initiate a chat with the chatbot.
  3. Ask Your Question: Once the chat starts, ask your question clearly. For example, "Give me information about crop diseases".
  4. Get Answers and Information: The chatbot will respond to your question and provide relevant information.
  5. End the Conversation: When you're finished, you can say "Thank You" or simply close the chat window.
